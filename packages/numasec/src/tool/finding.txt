Manage candidate and verified findings for the active operation.

Use this tool to:
- list current candidate and promoted findings;
- inspect a specific finding or candidate by key;
- promote a candidate into a finding with required evidence and optional replay;
- reject a candidate or finding when it is invalidated.

Promotion rules:
- a promoted finding must have evidence;
- replay is strongly preferred for active/reportable findings;
- if replay is absent, the finding remains evidence-backed but not replay-backed.
