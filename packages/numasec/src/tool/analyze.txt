Analyze a concrete cyber artifact or environment slice using the most appropriate first-party analyzer.

Use this semantic entrypoint when you want AppSec-oriented triage without deciding between raw tools yourself.

Targets:
- `iac` -> delegates to `iac_triage`
- `container` -> delegates to `container_surface`
- `cloud` -> delegates to `cloud_posture`
- `binary` -> delegates to `binary_triage`

The delegated tool remains responsible for evidence capture, graph population, provenance, and degradation when its adapter is missing.
