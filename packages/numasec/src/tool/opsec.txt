Toggle or inspect the operational security level of the active operation.

Args:
  - action: "status" (default) or "set"
  - level: "normal" | "strict" (required when action = "set")

Levels:
  - normal: the scope guard behaves as usual.
  - strict: the scope guard also refuses any traffic to known third-party
    intel hosts (crt.sh, otx.alienvault.com, www.virustotal.com,
    api.shodan.io, urlscan.io, wayback-api.archive.org) and upgrades
    unmatched "ask" decisions to "deny" so nothing leaks outside the
    declared target boundary (localhost excluded).

Use this before handling a sensitive engagement to reduce passive OSINT
leakage. Use action = "status" to read the current level and the list of
blocked hosts without changing anything.
